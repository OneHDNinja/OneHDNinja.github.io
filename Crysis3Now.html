Enter ultra realism
Videogames are a form of widespread, virtual recreation that can take the form of numerous different genres, types, and platforms. They are essentially millions of lines of code and calculations being executed at every second in order to display moving and interactive content on a display device. This form of gaming is becoming universal as more and more developing countries gain access to technology, as well an increasing amount of the older and new generation hopping on board the virtual gaming scene. Videogames have the capability of unifying humans by allowing us to play with individuals from a distance, and with those who come from different cultural and professional backgrounds. All in all, they help everyday people relieve themselves of stress, socialise with others online, and even make a career for themselves if they have a high enough skill level.
